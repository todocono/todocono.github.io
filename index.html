About me

Rudi

My full name is Rodolfo Cossovich, but I prefer to go by Rudi. I was born in Argentina and I lived big part of my adult life in China. I identify myself as male and hispanic. An image on this page displays a middle-aged person with little hair, a mustache, and a goat tee; smiling and holding a a robot.

I have solid technical expertise, ranging from consumer electronic product design to robotic systems. Nevertheless, I am deeply engaged in arts and social impact.

I am Assistant Arts Professor of Interactive Media Arts at NYU Shanghai, while I pursue remotely a PhD at Carleton University's Creative Intearaction Lab. My current research focus is Accessibility in Human-Computer Interaction.
